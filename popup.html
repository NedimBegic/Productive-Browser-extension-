    Daily Task

    Having a daily task is crucial because:

    • It provides structure and organization to your day.
    • Helps in personal growth and skill development.
    • Contributes to a sense of accomplishment and motivation.

    Night Review

    Having a night review is crucial because:

    • Reflect on the day's achievements and challenges for personal growth.
    • Identify areas for improvement and set goals for the next day.
    • Improve sleep quality by clearing your mind and reducing stress before bedtime.
